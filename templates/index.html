Question: What is the most significant threat that causes children death in US in recent years (2011-2015)

database: World Health Organization

http://apps.who.int/gho/data/view.main.ghe1002015-USA?lang=en

In this chart powered by Google Fusion Table, we could find that the biggest threat for newborn in US in recent years is the "Congenital anomalies". This lead the most biggest number of child death in total. Also, "Injuries" is another big threat for children in US. Although it did not cause many newborn death, it hurt children when they are in very-low age (1-month age to 4-year age). Given the serious situation of Injuries on low-age children, providing better Child Care serivce by government or NGO seems needed.
